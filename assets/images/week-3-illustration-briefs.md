# Week 3 Illustration Briefs — Pencil Sketch Style

Three pencil-style drawings for the symlink skills blog post. All should look hand-drawn, like whiteboard sketches with slight imperfections. Black pencil on white/cream background.

---

## 1. skill-symlink-architecture.png

**Composition:** Two rectangular boxes at the top (side by side), labeled "Claude Code" (left) and "Codex CLI" (right). Below them, centered, a cylinder/database shape labeled "~/.claude/skills/".

From the left box, a solid arrow goes straight down to the cylinder. From the right box, a dashed arrow goes down to a second small box labeled "~/.codex/skills/" with a symlink arrow (curved, with a chain-link icon) that curves back to the cylinder.

Inside the cylinder, show tiny folder icons: reflect, judge, ship, lancedb, frontend...

**Caption (handwritten style):** "One source of truth, two consumers."

**Mood:** Simple, architectural. Like a napkin sketch explaining the setup to a colleague.

---

## 2. skill-bridge-limits.png

**Composition:** Two islands connected by a simple rope bridge.

Left island labeled "Claude Code" has a tall tower made of stacked blocks. Each block labeled (bottom to top): CLAUDE.md, hooks, auto-memory, Living Rules, agents. The tower is tall and detailed.

Right island labeled "Codex" has a much simpler structure — just 2-3 blocks: config.toml, memories/ (with a "empty" label), trust levels.

The rope bridge between them is labeled "Symlinks". Small stick figures (skills) are walking across the bridge. But the tower blocks can't cross — they're too big/heavy for the bridge.

**Caption (handwritten style):** "Skills cross the bridge. Infrastructure doesn't."

**Mood:** Slightly whimsical but makes the serious point about what's portable vs what's locked-in.

---

## 3. portable-middle-layer.png

**Composition:** Three horizontal layers like a sandwich or layer cake, viewed from a slight 3/4 angle.

Top layer: Dark/shaded, labeled "Infrastructure" with a small padlock icon. Annotations on the side: "CLAUDE.md, hooks, auto-memory" (left) and "config.toml, memories/" (right).

Middle layer: Bright/highlighted, labeled "Skills" with bidirectional arrows pointing left and right off the edges. Small SKILL.md icons visible inside.

Bottom layer: Dark/shaded, labeled "Model" with small logos or text: "Opus", "Sonnet", "o3", "GPT-4.1".

A large arrow from the middle layer exits both sides, suggesting portability. The top and bottom layers have inward-facing arrows (locked in).

**Caption (handwritten style):** "The portable middle."

**Mood:** Clean, diagrammatic. The key insight in one image.
